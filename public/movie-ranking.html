The Best Movies According to Tom

My top movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

Spirited Away changed the landscape of animated film, being the only animated film to win an Oscar.

Arrival

Arrival is a mental gymnastics trip that is well worth a second watch.

Baby Driver

Baby Driver showed what a good soundtrack can do for a film. It adds extra layers of captivation in an enchanting way.

Interstellar

What's crazy about Interstellars being so wild, is that it's actually very scientifically accurate on much of it's theoretical portrayals.

Goodfellas

Martin Scorses created maybe the best gangster classic of all time. The telling of the story through self-narration was the absolute perfect choice here.

Wolf of Wall Street

Insanely vulgar; Insanely fun. My personal favorite follow-up of Goodfellas from Scorsese.

The Northman

A visual marvel of epic proportions with a very distinct style. It's definitely artsy and not for everyone, but it's worth a single watch for sure.

Top Gun: Maverick

The extremely rare case of a long awaited sequel that somehow surpasses the original and without ruining the original groundwork for old fans.

Mission: Impossible - Dead Reckoning Part One

Somehow Tom Cruise followed up the insane stunts of Top Gun: Maverick with even more wild stunts. At this point I'm worried his adrenaline-junkie inner-self is going to lead to a sad fate.

The Dark Knight

Heath Ledger gave the performance of a lifetime as The Joker and proved it's possible to truly take the lead role as a Villain in entertainment.

The Goonies

A childhood favorite of mine that is part of the peak 80s feel-good movies many grew up on and with.
